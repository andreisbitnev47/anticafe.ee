Our concept

Anticafe is a contemporary entertainment format that only costs time and everything is free of charge. Here you can relax with friends, celebrate birthday parties, meet with meetings, organize training sessions and conferences

Our cafe offers game consoles, board games and table tennis, as well as tea and cookies. If you wish, you can bring in or order a meal.

Anticafe has low prices, the cost per minute is only 5 cents.

Our cafe has 4 rooms for different types of entertainment that can be booked.
